Welcome to Mines-vs-Treasure game

Simplified explanation

This game is a little different than usual. Bombs will reduce your points and tell you whether you are close to the treasure or not.

Please select a difficulty level

Easy level

There will be 10 bombs on the way, and if you press the bomb, you will lose 5 points

Hard level

There will be 20 bombs on the way, and if you press the bomb, you will lose 7 points
