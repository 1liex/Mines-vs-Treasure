Welcome to Mines-vs-Treasure game

Simplified explanation

This game is a little different than usual. Bombs will reduce your points and On the Hard level, your chances will be reduced by 2.

Please select a difficulty level

Easy level

There will be 12 bombs on the way, and if you press the bomb, you will lose 5 points

Hard level

There will be 24 bombs on the way, and if you press the bomb, you will lose 8 points
